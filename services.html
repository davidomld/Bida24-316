Our Comprehensive Jewelry Services

At Gemmes et Bijoux, our commitment to excellence extends beyond our exquisite collections. We offer a full spectrum of professional services to preserve the beauty and value of your precious jewelry.

Custom Jewelry Design

Collaborate with our master jewelers to transform your unique vision into a tangible masterpiece. From initial sketches to the final polish, we guide you through every step of creating a bespoke piece that tells your story.

Expert Jewelry Repair & Restoration

Trust your cherished heirlooms and everyday favorites to our skilled artisans. We specialize in intricate repairs, gemstone resetting, clasp replacements, and full historical restorations, ensuring your pieces return to their original splendor.

Professional Cleaning & Maintenance

Restore the brilliant sparkle to your jewelry with our meticulous cleaning services. We also provide thorough inspections to identify any potential issues early, helping to prevent future damage and ensure longevity.

Certified Appraisals & Valuations

Obtain accurate and legally recognized appraisals for insurance purposes, estate planning, or resale. Our certified gemologists conduct detailed examinations and provide comprehensive documentation for your valuable assets.

Personalized Engraving Services

Add a deeply personal touch to your jewelry with our precision engraving services. Commemorate special dates, initials, or meaningful messages, making your gift truly unforgettable and unique.

Ethical Gemstone Sourcing

Whether for a new design or a replacement, we offer expert sourcing of high-quality, ethically obtained gemstones from around the world. We commit to transparency and responsible practices in all our material acquisitions.
