Our Services

At Gemmes et Bijoux, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet all your jewelry needs, from custom design to meticulous repair.

Custom Design

Bring your unique vision to life. Our skilled designers work with you to create personalized jewelry, from engagement rings to custom pendants, ensuring every detail is perfect.

Jewelry Repair & Restoration

Trust your cherished pieces to our expert artisans. We offer comprehensive repair, resizing, and restoration services to bring your jewelry back to its original beauty.

Appraisals & Certification

Obtain professional appraisals and certifications for your diamonds and gemstones. Our certified gemologists provide accurate valuations for insurance or resale purposes.

Cleaning & Polishing

Restore the sparkle and brilliance of your jewelry with our professional cleaning and polishing services, ensuring your pieces always look their best.

Engraving

Add a personal touch to your jewelry with our precision engraving services. Perfect for special dates, names, or meaningful messages.

Gemstone Sourcing

Looking for a specific gemstone? We specialize in ethically sourcing rare and high-quality diamonds and colored gemstones from around the world.
